Device Features and Bugs

Listed here are the working, partially working and non-working features related to all Android Pie AOSP ROMs for the Samsung Galaxy S7 and S7 edge

Working features

- Bluetooth
- Buttons (functionality and backlight)
- Camera
- Device sensors
- Fingerprint sensor
- GPS
- GPU acceleration (2D and 3D)
- Graphics
- Hardware encoding / decoding
- Hotspot
- MTP
- Offline charging (charging whilst powered off)
- RIL (messages, calls and data)
- Sound
- USB tethering
- Hardware Composer (HWC)
- Video playback
- Wi-Fi

Non-working

- VoLTE (most likely will never work)
